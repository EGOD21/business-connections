Create a new note in the Mast or Anchor folder for each CRM contact filled out with any pertinent information. 

To link it to another contact in graph view make sure to click "Command + P" and type in "Internal Note" then type the contact you want it to connect to. 

To have a note published on the public site then click "Command + P" again and type in "Digital Garden: Publish Single Note" then click enter. Wait a few minutes then you'll see the change at: https://business-connections.vercel.app/
